Our Inspiration

My journey with quotes started long before Cita was conceived. Growing up, I found solace and inspiration in the words of great thinkers and writers. During a particularly challenging period in high school, when I was struggling to find my own voice amidst the noise of expectations and self-doubt, I stumbled upon a quote by Ralph Waldo Emerson: "To be yourself in a world that is constantly trying to make you something else is the greatest accomplishment." This quote resonated deeply with me and became a source of strength and motivation. Years later, as a software engineer, I wanted to create something that could help others find similar moments of clarity and inspiration. Cita is not just a project; it's a manifestation of my personal journey with words and the impact they can have on one's life. My primary focus was to ensure a cohesive project flow and develop a robust frontend application. I aimed to create a responsive design that is both web and mobile-friendly, making it accessible for users on different devices. Additionally, I ensured that the integration between the frontend and backend was seamless, providing a smooth user experience from quote discovery to sharing.

Key Features

Daily Quote

A daily quote feature that provides users with a new quote every day on the homepage.

Responsive Design

The application is fully responsive and accessible on both web and mobile devices.

Admin Panel

An admin panel for managing the platform, including reviewing user-contributed quotes, monitoring API integrations, and managing user accounts.
